About Me

I transferred from Bellevue College in the Fall of 2012 planning to get a degree in Psychology. Half way through my first quarter, I realized Psychology wasn't the right major for me. I saw that Technical Communication taught writing in the workplace. I signed up for Tech Comm classes Winter quarter and haven't regretted it for a second. I've loved my classes, and I'm so excited to apply everything I've learned in a career. This last Fall, I completed my final quarter at Eastern Washington University. I have since returned to Seattle to begin a career in writing using my degree.

Professional Objective

I want to develop content for print and web for non technical industries. Now that I've taken design classes, I want that included in my job description. With the writing, editing, design, journalism, and communication skills I've gained during this degree, my career options are endless. Because of the various concentrations I've studied, I can fit into many different Editing roles.
