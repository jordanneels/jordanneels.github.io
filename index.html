A Little Bit About Me

After growing up in the Seattle area and attending Bellevue College for my Associate's degree, I transferred to Eastern Washington University to earn my Bachelor's degree in Technical Communication. This came with a built-in Visual Communication Design minor which has made me appreciate and enjoy graphic and web design.

Writer. Designer. Editor

In December 2014, I graduated from EWU and returned to the rainy city of Seattle. I'm excited to apply my passion of writing, editing, and graphic and web design to a career!
